About Us

Bringing Sunlight to Every Home in Kashmir

Solterra Energy was founded with a single purpose: making clean, affordable solar power accessible to every family in Jammu & Kashmir. Born from the valleys of Kashmir, we understand the unique energy challenges of our region — from power cuts to rising electricity bills.

Our certified team of engineers and installation experts work closely with every homeowner, from site assessment to commissioning — ensuring you get maximum solar output and maximum savings. We are an authorized installer under the PM Surya Ghar Yojana, meaning you get all government benefits handled end-to-end.

🎯 Our Mission

To make sustainable solar energy the affordable, first choice for every home — creating a greener Kashmir, one rooftop at a time.

Government Scheme

PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana

The Government of India has launched a landmark scheme to put solar panels on 1 crore homes. As an approved installer, Solterra Energy helps you claim every rupee of subsidy you deserve.

₹94,800

Maximum subsidy for residential systems — directly credited to your bank account by the government.

1

Free Site Survey

Our expert visits your home, checks your roof and electricity usage, and recommends the right solar system.

2

Subsidy Application

We handle all paperwork and registration on the national portal — PM Surya Ghar — on your behalf.

3

Installation in Days

Certified installation completed within 3–5 working days. Panels, inverter, wiring — all done by our team.

4

Subsidy in Your Account

After inspection by the DISCOM, the government transfers your subsidy amount directly to your bank account.
